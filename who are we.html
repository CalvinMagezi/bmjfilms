About La Jefa

Bad Mama Jama Films was born in April 2019, by disco loving colour fiend Nicole Magabo Kiggundu. Nicole writes, directs and produces (not always at the same time) our films in development. She is a proud graduate of the Medill School of Journalism at Northwestern University and The London Film School. Find her on Twitter overusing emojis @nicolemagabo.
